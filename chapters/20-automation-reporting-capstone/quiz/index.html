    Quiz: Automation, Reporting, and Capstone Systems¶

    Test your understanding of automation, reporting, and capstone systems with these review questions.

    1. Which definition best describes Capstone System?¶

    1. A graph node representing one evaluation standard and linking it to rubrics, scores, evidence, and findings.
    2. A durable, searchable record of findings, evidence, decisions, and lessons that remains available beyond one project or team.
    3. The connected stages that move an asset from intake through persona reviews, synthesis, validation, and reporting.
    4. The complete course project that integrates personas, prompts, agents, evaluation, evidence, graphs, and reporting into one working process.
    Show Answer

    The correct answer is D. The complete course project that integrates personas, prompts, agents, evaluation, evidence, graphs, and reporting into one working process. Recognizing the precise definition prevents the term from being substituted for a nearby but different idea. The alternatives instead point toward Criterion Node, Organizational Memory, Evaluation Pipeline, or detach the concept from the evidence and decision it is meant to support. Keeping those ideas distinct helps the team state its method, claim, and limits explicitly.
