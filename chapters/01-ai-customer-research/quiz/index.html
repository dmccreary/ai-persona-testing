    1. Which definition best describes Model Instructions?¶

    1. Computer systems that perform tasks associated with human reasoning, language, perception, or decision-making by applying learned patterns and explicit instructions.
    2. The limited amount of tokenized information a language model can consider during one interaction, including instructions, inputs, and prior messages.
    3. Directions supplied to an AI system that define its role, task, constraints, priorities, or expected form of response.
    4. Structured, AI-operable customer profiles that combine relevant traits, goals, circumstances, and behaviors for repeatable simulated evaluation.
    Show Answer

    The correct answer is C. Directions supplied to an AI system that define its role, task, constraints, priorities, or expected form of response. Recognizing the precise definition prevents the term from being substituted for a nearby but different idea. The alternatives instead point toward Artificial Intelligence, Model Context Window, Synthetic Personas, or detach the concept from the evidence and decision it is meant to support. Keeping those ideas distinct helps the team state its method, claim, and limits explicitly.
