    Quiz: Customer Segmentation and Persona Archetypes¶

    Test your understanding of customer segmentation and persona archetypes with these review questions.

    1. Which definition best describes Needs-Based Segmentation?¶

    1. The division of a broad market into groups whose meaningful differences support distinct research, messaging, product, or service decisions.
    2. Grouping customers by their economic value to the organization or by the value they seek from an offer.
    3. Grouping customers by the problems, required benefits, or desired outcomes they are trying to address.
    4. A profile of someone the offering should not target, used to prevent wasted effort and misleading broad appeal.
    Show Answer

    The correct answer is C. Grouping customers by the problems, required benefits, or desired outcomes they are trying to address. Recognizing the precise definition prevents the term from being substituted for a nearby but different idea. The alternatives instead point toward Customer Segmentation, Value-Based Segmentation, Negative Persona, or detach the concept from the evidence and decision it is meant to support. Keeping those ideas distinct helps the team state its method, claim, and limits explicitly.
