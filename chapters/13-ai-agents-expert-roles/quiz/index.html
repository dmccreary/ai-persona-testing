    Quiz: Designing AI Agents and Expert Roles¶

    Test your understanding of designing ai agents and expert roles with these review questions.

    1. Which definition best describes AI Agent?¶

    1. The perspective and responsibility assigned to an agent, defining the kind of contribution it should make.
    2. Information retained across steps or sessions so an agent can use earlier facts, decisions, or results.
    3. Explicit limits on an agent’s allowed actions, data access, claims, decisions, and escalation authority.
    4. An AI participant that receives a defined role, goal, context, and boundaries, then performs assigned evaluation work with a specified degree of autonomy.
    Show Answer

    The correct answer is D. An AI participant that receives a defined role, goal, context, and boundaries, then performs assigned evaluation work with a specified degree of autonomy. Recognizing the precise definition prevents the term from being substituted for a nearby but different idea. The alternatives instead point toward Agent Role, Agent Memory, Agent Boundaries, or detach the concept from the evidence and decision it is meant to support. Keeping those ideas distinct helps the team state its method, claim, and limits explicitly.
