    Quiz: Prompt Engineering Foundations¶

    Test your understanding of prompt engineering foundations with these review questions.

    1. Which definition best describes Constraint Definition?¶

    1. An explicit boundary on content, behavior, evidence, scope, format, or permitted action.
    2. The deliberate design and testing of instructions, context, examples, and output requirements to improve an AI system’s task performance.
    3. The immediate request, question, or source material submitted for the AI system to address within higher-priority rules and available context.
    4. Instructions that direct an agent to challenge claims, inspect risks, and identify unsupported assumptions without becoming indiscriminately negative.
    Show Answer

    The correct answer is A. An explicit boundary on content, behavior, evidence, scope, format, or permitted action. Recognizing the precise definition prevents the term from being substituted for a nearby but different idea. The alternatives instead point toward Prompt Engineering, User Prompt, Skeptic Prompt, or detach the concept from the evidence and decision it is meant to support. Keeping those ideas distinct helps the team state its method, claim, and limits explicitly.
