    Quiz: AI Capabilities, Limitations, and Bias¶

    Test your understanding of ai capabilities, limitations, and bias with these review questions.

    1. Which definition best describes Persona Realism?¶

    1. Tasks an AI system can perform adequately under stated conditions, such as generating, classifying, comparing, or summarizing content.
    2. Plausible-sounding content generated without adequate factual support in the supplied evidence or reliable knowledge.
    3. A tendency to seek, interpret, or remember information in ways that favor an existing belief or desired conclusion.
    4. The degree to which a persona’s characteristics and responses form a plausible, evidence-grounded representation of a customer.
    Show Answer

    The correct answer is D. The degree to which a persona’s characteristics and responses form a plausible, evidence-grounded representation of a customer. Recognizing the precise definition prevents the term from being substituted for a nearby but different idea. The alternatives instead point toward AI Capabilities, Model Hallucination, Confirmation Bias, or detach the concept from the evidence and decision it is meant to support. Keeping those ideas distinct helps the team state its method, claim, and limits explicitly.
