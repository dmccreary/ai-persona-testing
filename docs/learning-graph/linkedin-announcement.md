# LinkedIn Announcement — AI Persona Testing

## Full Version

I’m excited to share **AI Persona Testing**, a free interactive intelligent textbook for marketing professionals—and a milestone in how we build open educational resources.

This is the first full interactive intelligent textbook we have generated with an OpenAI model rather than Anthropic Claude. Built with OpenAI’s newest GPT-5.5 model—available in ChatGPT and Codex—using the Codex desktop app, it gave us a valuable test of whether our skill-driven textbook workflow could transfer cleanly across model providers while preserving structure, pedagogy, and quality.

The book teaches marketers, brand strategists, UX researchers, consultants, and business leaders how to design repeatable AI persona testing systems without programming. It covers evidence quality, responsible AI, persona design, prompt testing, multi-agent workflows, evaluation rubrics, knowledge graphs, and automated reporting.

📊 By the numbers:
• 20 chapters
• 400 concepts organized in a prerequisite-aware learning graph
• 82 interactive MicroSims and 81 diagrams
• 200 quiz questions across 20 chapter quizzes
• 400 glossary terms and 82 FAQs
• 153,530 words—about 675 printed pages

The larger lesson is encouraging: a carefully designed system of reusable skills, source-of-truth metrics, validation checks, and human direction can make an intelligent-textbook workflow portable across frontier models. The model matters, but so does the process wrapped around it.

The full textbook is free to explore. Link in the first comment below. 👇
