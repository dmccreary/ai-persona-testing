4. The observable population attributes such as age range, location, or household type.
</div>

??? question "Show Answer"
    The correct answer is **B**. The observable actions and routines relevant to the buying situation. Recognizing the precise definition prevents the term from being substituted for a nearby but different idea. The alternatives instead point toward Customer Persona, Persona Identity, Persona Demographics, or detach the concept from the evidence and decision it is meant to support. Keeping those ideas distinct helps the team state its method, claim, and limits explicitly.
