## About This MicroSim

This explorer differentiates customer simulation, agent-based simulation, AI focus groups, and brand stress testing. Selecting a mode redraws the participant structure and explains the evidence the mode can reasonably produce.

## How to Use

Select or click a mode, pin two modes for comparison, then choose a research scenario and test which mode best fits. Read the structural and evidence explanation rather than choosing by name alone.

## Lesson Plan

### Grade Level
Undergraduate and professional marketing research

### Duration
10-15 minutes

### Prerequisites
Basic familiarity with personas, customer research, and research objectives.

### Activities

1. **Explore** (4 min): Inspect all four participant structures.
2. **Compare** (5 min): Pin two modes and explain how their evidence differs.
3. **Apply** (5 min): Match each scenario to the best mode and defend the choice.

### Assessment
Learners can differentiate the four modes using purpose, number of agents, interaction pattern, and expected evidence.
