## About This MicroSim

This MicroSim helps learners distinguish customer research from market research and qualitative evidence from quantitative evidence. Ten realistic marketing questions reveal why a method fits and when a mixed-method extension would be stronger.

## How to Use

Read the question, select the method that would produce the strongest first evidence, and choose **Check classification**. Use **Show another defensible answer** to examine ambiguity, then continue to the next question.

## Lesson Plan

### Grade Level
Undergraduate and professional marketing education

### Duration
10-15 minutes

### Prerequisites
Basic familiarity with qualitative and quantitative research.

### Activities

1. **Predict** (3 min): Classify the first three questions before requesting feedback.
2. **Practice** (7 min): Complete the remaining scenarios and explain what evidence each method would produce.
3. **Reflect** (5 min): Select two ambiguous questions and propose mixed-method sequences.

### Assessment
Learners should correctly classify at least eight scenarios and justify choices using the evidence needed rather than the surface wording of the question.
