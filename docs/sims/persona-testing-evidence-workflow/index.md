## About This MicroSim

This three-lane workflow separates marketing-team inputs, persona-system analysis, and human-research validation. Each stage exposes its input, action, output, and quality check so synthetic hypotheses do not quietly become validated claims.

## How to Use

Choose a scenario, inspect each workflow step, then place evidence artifacts into their proper stages. Incorrect placements explain the correct handoff; completed stages turn green.

## Lesson Plan

### Grade Level
Undergraduate and professional marketing research

### Duration
10-15 minutes

### Prerequisites
Basic familiarity with customer personas, qualitative research, and evidence limitations.

### Activities

1. **Trace** (4 min): Click through the nine stages and name each handoff.
2. **Practice** (7 min): Place all evidence artifacts for one scenario.
3. **Transfer** (5 min): Explain which synthetic claims require real-customer validation and why.

### Assessment
Learners correctly place the artifacts and produce a validation decision that clearly distinguishes synthetic hypotheses from externally verified evidence.
