# Image Prompts for Lens

I am about to ask you to generate seven different poses for a book mascot.
Please use a consistent drawing style for all seven images.

Every image should be generated at 1024×1024. The chroma-key source should be
converted to an RGBA PNG with a fully transparent alpha-channel background,
trimmed to the visible character, and optimized to less than 100 KB when
practical.

## 1. Neutral — `neutral.png`

Use case: scientific-educational. Asset type: pedagogical mascot pose for an
interactive textbook. Create Lens, an anthropomorphic magnifying-glass research
guide. Lens has a large circular blue-grey rim (`#546E7A`) containing a solid
pale-blue face (`#E1F5FE`), a compact deep-orange handle-body (`#FF5722`), two
slender blue-grey arms ending in simple four-fingered hands, and two small
stable feet. Lens has calm, curious eyes and a subtle friendly closed-mouth
smile. No clothing and no accessories. Both hands must be empty and fully
visible. Polished modern flat-vector illustration, clean dark outlines, simple
geometric forms, minimal shading, professional educational tone, centered
full-character composition, no text. Lens stands facing the viewer in a
relaxed neutral pose. Both arms rest naturally at the sides and neither hand
makes a specific gesture.

Create the subject on a perfectly flat solid `#00FF00` chroma-key background
for background removal. The background must be one uniform color with no
shadows, gradients, texture, floor plane, reflections, or lighting variation.
Do not use green anywhere in Lens. No cast shadow, contact shadow, watermark,
speech bubble, notebook, clipboard, pointer, or handheld object.

## Post-processing

For each generated source image, replace the chroma-key background with
transparency and then trim any remaining transparent padding. Verify that the
corners have alpha `0`, the visible character fills the canvas appropriately,
and no green fringe remains around the blue-grey arms or hands.
