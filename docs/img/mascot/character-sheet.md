# Character Sheet: Lens the Evidence Explorer

The canonical identity document for Lens, the pedagogical mascot for the
**AI Persona Testing** textbook. Every pose prompt and every piece of
AI-generated content involving Lens must re-anchor to this description. It is
the source of truth for visual and voice consistency.

## Identity

- **Name:** Lens
- **Type:** Anthropomorphic magnifying glass
- **Role:** Evidence-focused research guide
- **Subject:** AI-assisted customer research and persona testing for marketing professionals
- **Catchphrase:** "Let's look closer."
- **Pronouns:** They/them

## Visual Description

- **Primary color:** Blue grey — hex `#546E7A`
- **Accent color:** Deep orange — hex `#FF5722`
- **Face color:** Pale blue — hex `#E1F5FE`
- **Form:** A circular blue-grey magnifying-glass rim containing a pale-blue face, with a deep-orange handle forming the compact body
- **Arms and hands:** Two slender blue-grey arms with simple four-fingered hands; both hands always remain free for expressive gestures
- **Clothing / accessories:** None; Lens never carries a notebook, clipboard, pointer, or other handheld object
- **Expression:** Calm, curious eyes and a subtle, friendly closed-mouth smile
- **Size proportion:** Compact, icon-friendly character with a large circular head, short handle-body, slim arms, and small stable feet
- **Art style:** Polished modern flat-vector illustration with clean dark outlines, simple geometric forms, and minimal shading

## Personality

- Analytical without sounding clinical
- Calm and methodical
- Curious about evidence and missing perspectives
- Supportive while remaining appropriately skeptical

## Voice

- Uses brief, plain-language questions that improve the reader's reasoning
- Clearly distinguishes observation, evidence, inference, hypothesis, and conclusion
- Challenges unsupported certainty without scolding the reader
- Never speaks as a customer, a source of customer evidence, or an all-knowing AI
- Signature phrases: "Let's look closer.", "What supports that?", "Is that evidence or inference?", "What might we be missing?"

## Why This Mascot

A magnifying glass directly represents examination, evidence, and disciplined
inquiry without personifying an AI system or pretending to be a synthetic
customer. Lens is visually distinctive across the intelligent-textbook
collection, while the blue-grey and deep-orange palette fits this book's
existing theme and preserves a professional tone for working marketers.
